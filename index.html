MOTORSPORT ASSOCIATION OF PAKISTAN

The Motorsport Association of Pakistan (MAP) is a member of the Federation Internationale de L’Automobile (FIA). MAP’s primary responsibility is the governance of automotive sport in Pakistan under the International Sporting Code of the FIA. Globally, motorsport is governed by the FIA.

WHAT WE DO

The FIA has invested MAP with sole authority to exercise the sporting power as the National Sporting Authority (ASN) for motorsports in Pakistan with the object of regulating all forms of motorsports. MAP has been entrusted to enforce all official FIA statutes, regulations and their enclosures within the territory of Pakistan.

MISSION / OBJECTIVE

Exercise the sporting power as “ASN” for Pakistan in accordance with the International Sporting Code and such instructions and directions as may be received from the FIA for all forms of motorsport.
